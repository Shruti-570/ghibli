Food

Rice Balls (Onigiri):

A Japanese snack made of rice shaped into triangles or ovals, often with fillings like salmon or pickled plum, and wrapped in seaweed. Simple, tasty, and perfect on the go.

Pancakes

Fluffy, golden rounds of comfort—often stacked and topped with syrup, butter, or fruit. A breakfast classic loved around the world.

Orange Tea

A bright, citrusy blend of tea and orange peel. Refreshing hot or cold, it’s a cozy drink with a hint of sunshine.

ABOUT ME

Shruti is a dreamer, storyteller, and collector of little joys. She finds magic in quiet mornings, misty forests, and the soft rustle of pages in well-loved books. Inspired by Studio Ghibli’s worlds, she creates her own—filled with cozy corners, gentle creatures, and comforting foods like warm rice balls and sweet pancakes. Whether she's sketching a forest spirit or sipping orange tea under a rainy sky, Shruti believes in the beauty of slow living and everyday wonder.
